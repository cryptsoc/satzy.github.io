KETO & PALEO MENU RECOMMENDATIONS

We understand that eating well might mean something different to each of us. That’s why our signature dishes can be customized countless ways to meet your dietary need or preference. We have a variety of dishes that work well for the keto or paleo lifestyle. Here’s a list of dishes and modifications to fit the suggested dietary restrictions.

Fall 2019

Keto-Friendly

Starters

  • Kale Guacamole
    no pita chips, substitute vegetables
  • Seasonal Vegetable Crudités
    as is
  • Roasted Brussels Sprouts
    no miso vinaigrette, substitute kale salad dressing

Salads

  • Organic Tuscan Kale
    no bread crumbs, add avocado
  • Good Earth Kale Cobb
    no pickled watermelon radish, no corn, no garbanzo bean, substitute plain radish, extra brussels sprout, avocado & tomato
  • Seasonal Ingredient Salad
    no bean, no butternut squash, add extra cauliflower & brussels sprout, no pomegranate seed, no mulberry, no horseradish vinaigrette, substitute kale salad dressing

Sandwiches

  • (order kale salad as your side with no breadcrumbs)
  • Grass-fed Burger
    no bun, substitute lettuce wrap, no umami
  • Turkey Burger
    no bun, substitute lettuce wrap, no jalapeño remoulade

Entrées

  • Mediterranean Chicken
    no quinoa, no peppadew pepper, no lemon oregano vinaigrette, substitutekale salad dressing
  • Spaghetti Squash Casserole
    the carbs in this dish come from the squash and onion. Depending on your carbohydrate restriction, you may want to order a ½ order with extra cheese, vegan almond ricotta, avocado or cilantro pumpkin seed pesto. Recommendation: add Grass-fed Burger or Turkey Burger
  • Grilled Salmon
    no grain, substitute kale salad dressing on arugula & beet salad

Brunch

    as is

Sauces/Dressings

  • Recommended keto-friendly alternatives:

Keto Tips

  • Our grilled salmon and grass-fed beef are delicious, full of healthy fat & are excellent keto-friendly additions to any of our dishes.
  • Whenever possible, add extra healthy fat to dishes. Examples of ingredients containing healthy fats are avocado, lemon ricotta, extra cheese, or a side of pesto or kale salad dressing.
  • If you are eliminating dairy as part of a keto diet, our vegan almond ricotta or avocado are good alternatives.
  • For all other dietary restrictions or allergies, please ask your server for further assistance.

  • Turkey Burger
    no bun, substitute lettuce wrap, no cheese, no jalapeño remoulade
