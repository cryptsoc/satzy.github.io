Private Dining & Events

True Food Kitchen at Scottsdale Quarter does not feature a private event space, however large groups are always welcome and the restaurant and patio are both available for buy-outs. Located in the heart of Scottsdale Quarter sits True Food Kitchen, with its gorgeous patio views of this open-air shopping destination’s main courtyard. Guests can have their gathering seated on the wrap around outdoor patio complete with a community fire pit and bistro lighting while the inside dining space is perfect for a night out for two, cocktail parties or more formal business dinners. True Food Kitchen can host sit down dinners with multiple seating configurations or reception-style events. Indoor and outdoor spaces can be combined by opening the glass doors of the patio to form one large event space for large events and buy-outs.

Seating Capacity
• Exclusive patio space: up to 130 guests seated.
• Open to full restaurant buy outs.
• Multiple seating configurations.
